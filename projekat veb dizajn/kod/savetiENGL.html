How to clean ice from a car?

With the arrival of cold days, drivers encounter icy windows / doors. In today's text, we provide useful tips that save time and nerves, especially in the morning when you go to work. If you do not have time to start the engine and wait for the heating to defrost the glass, you must prepare a strategy in advance. Leave the wipers raised overnight (so that the tire does not stick), and put cardboard or a cloth over the windshield. This is to prevent the accumulation of ice and make the job easier. Without these actions, you will spend a lot of time and effort to clean the glass. Never pour hot water on the windshield to prevent it from cracking, and you can make the cleaning fluid yourself. Pour 2/3 of medical alcohol, 1/3 of warm water and a little dish detergent into a plastic bottle with a spray bottle. Apply the resulting mixture on the glass. This combination will protect you from icing, and the snow will not stay. Spray the windows in the evening and there will be no problems in the morning.

How to properly store summer tires during the winter season?

Most drivers replaced their summer tires and probably found themselves in a problem with storage. If you do not have enough space, look for the cheapest "tire hotel" and thus provide them with accommodation during the winter. If you decide to take care of your tires yourself, here are some important tips for maintaining quality and saving money. First clean them, remove stones and other "intruders" from the surface. It is best to store tires in dark, dry and cold rooms, because heat and UV rays can damage them. Try not to come into contact with fuel, grease, paints and other chemicals, reports HAK Magazine. If you store them together with the rims, feel free to reduce the air pressure, but not too much. Store them in an upright position to avoid loads and possible deformations
